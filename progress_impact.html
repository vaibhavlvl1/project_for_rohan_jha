4. Awards and Annual Fest

For continuous motivation, we started monthly three awards for main school and two for elementary school: Sample submissions of write-ups and some names. Similarly we will have an annual academic festival on the occasion of Teachers' day, we'll organize an event in five areas in both schools: Math, science, essay writing, speech, and debate.
